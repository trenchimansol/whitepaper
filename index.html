Executive Summary

Trenchi represents a revolutionary approach to social connection in the cryptocurrency space, specifically designed for the vibrant community of memecoin traders on the Solana blockchain.

In a digital landscape where traditional dating applications fail to understand the unique lifestyle of cryptocurrency enthusiasts, Trenchi emerges as the first decentralized matching platform that connects individuals based on their trading performance, shared interests, and compatible risk profiles.

The Trenchi ecosystem is powered by its native TRENCHI token and features an exclusive collection of 10 limited-edition NFTs that provide lifetime platform access and special privileges to their holders.
